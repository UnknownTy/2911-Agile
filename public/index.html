What is a Discord Bot?

  • A discord bot is a robot that is programmed to answer to different commands. The bot is based on discord messenging platform.

    • Why Trust our Covid Data Bot

  • Our Covid data bot uses information from trust worthy websites such as disease.sh API to get the statistics and we will get info and refer you to websites from the government websites

    • What are the feature can Covid Data Bot do?

  • Can give you daily announcements from the BC govenment
  • Will be able to give you the statistics of covid numbers per country and world
  • Information links on where to register for the vaccine
  • Show the restrictions per region
  • Tells you if you are eligible for the Vaccine
  • Provides links to information on the Vaccine

    • The Commands the Covid Bot can answer:

  • !stat [Country]: Will give you the statistics of COVID numbers in the specific country
  • !staty [Country] : Will give the statistics of COVID numbers yesterday in the specific country
  • !when [age] : will let you know when you can get vaccinated
  • !help : will give you the available commands
  • !prefix : updates the prefix
  • !restriction [reigion] : Gives you the basic information on restrictions and a link
  • !info [vaccine]: will give you a link to the vaccine
  • !register [province abbreviation] : will give you the link where you can register to get vacinated
  • !faq : most frequently asked question
