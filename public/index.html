Welcome to our COVID Data Bot!

What is a Discord Bot?

  • A discord bot is a robot that is programmed to answer to different commands. The bot is based on discord messenging platform.

Why trust our Covid Data Bot

  • Our Covid data bot uses information from reliable websites such as disease.sh API to get the statistics and we will get info and refer you to websites from the government websites

Features

  • Returns daily announcements from the BC govenment
  • Will be able to give you the statistics of covid numbers per country and around the world
  • Information links on where to register for the vaccine
  • Show the restrictions per region
  • Tells you if you are eligible for the Vaccine
  • Provides links to information on the Vaccine

Various Commands the Covid Bot can respond to:

  • !stat [Country]: Will give you the statistics of COVID numbers in the specific country
  • !staty [Country] : Will give the statistics of COVID numbers yesterday in the specific country
  • !when [age] : determines when you can get your first vaccine
  • !help : displays the proper usage for a given command
  • !prefix : updates the prefix
  • !restriction [reigion] : Gives you the basic information on restrictions and a link
  • !info [vaccine]: Information on individual vaccines
  • !register [province abbreviation] : Provides the link where you can register to get vaccinated
  • !faq : Frequently asked questions
